Email Management

Manage your custom email addresses and routing with Cloudflare.

Svart Suite Email Addresses

Reach us using any of the following official email addresses:

Address Purpose
support@svartsecurity.org Customer support & help
contact@svartsecurity.org General inquiries
security@svartsecurity.org Security vulnerability reports
admin@svartsecurity.org Admin & internal use
noreply@svartsecurity.org Automated emails (no replies)

Cloudflare Email Routing Setup

All email addresses above are routed through Cloudflare Email Routing to forward mail to your inbox.

How to configure:

  1. Go to Cloudflare Dashboard → select svartsecurity.org.
  2. Navigate to Email > Email Routing.
  3. Click Create address and add each address above.
  4. Set the forwarding destination (your personal inbox).
  5. Verify the destination email if prompted.

Note: Sending email from these addresses requires Cloudflare's email sending feature (currently in beta waitlist). Once enabled, outbound emails will work automatically.
