How It Works

A security setup where you're actually in charge — not us.

Look, I'm just one person trying to fix something that's broken. Everything here is built so that you hold the keys, not me. I literally can't give away what I never held.

— Jaeger, Founder of Svart Security

The Building Blocks

🔑

Your Secret Key

Your secret key is the only thing we keep. It gets you into your web dashboard to download apps or update your info. That's it — we never see your actual passwords. Can't leak what we don't have.

🔒

Password Recovery

Forget your password? We verify through your key and email — but we never see your actual passwords. A temporary reset gets you back in, then you set a new one locally.

🌐

Self-Hosted Chat

Your chat server goes wherever you want — your basement, a friend's place, wherever. Our build packager just gives it an ID so it connects. Your messages stay on your hardware.

💻

Local-Only Login

Log into apps with your secret key plus a local password that never leaves your device. No cloud sync, no server storage — your credentials exist only where you type them.

🛡

The Vault App

Want to store passwords safely? We're building a Vault app for that — completely local. Your secrets stay on your machine, encrypted and under your control.

🛡

Network Guardian

Network Guardian lives inside every app, not on your server. It monitors network activity locally and only flags what actually needs reporting — no mass surveillance, no unnecessary data collection.

🚨

Panic Button

Set a duress key that wipes everything if you need it. If you're ever in a situation where your data needs to disappear, one code makes it happen. No questions, no delays.

☁

Svart AI — Online Only

Svart AI needs an internet connection to work — it talks to cloud models behind the scenes. Everything else in the suite runs locally, but AI can't run offline. We'd rather be honest about that than pretend otherwise.
