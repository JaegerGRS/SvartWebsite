Powerful Features

Everything you need for private, secure browsing

🔒

Advanced Encryption & Security

All your data is protected with AES-256-GCM encryption, Argon2 password hashing, HMAC authentication, and SHA-256 integrity checks. Vaults and passwords are always stored locally—never sent to any server. Your privacy and security are built-in by design.

🚫

Built-in Ad Blocker

Ad blocking powered by uBlock Origin filter lists and community.
"Finally, an efficient blocker. Easy on CPU and memory."
Credit to uBlock Origin for their outstanding open-source work and commitment to user freedom and privacy.

🔑

Alias & Forwarding Email Support

For maximum privacy, use an alias or forwarding email for all online accounts. Your Svart ID is your only identity—no email or personal data is stored.

🌐

NextDNS Privacy Partner

On first login, you’ll be prompted to set up a secure DNS provider. DNS-over-HTTPS with NextDNS is recommended for encrypted DNS queries and additional protection. We promote NextDNS as a privacy tool (not a sponsor); our link is an affiliate link, which helps support Svart Security at no extra cost to you. We only recommend it because it is the best privacy DNS for most users.

📱

Cross-Platform

Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux. Take your privacy with you everywhere.

🧪

Chromium & Dev Mode

Svart Browser is built on Chromium for maximum compatibility and performance. In Dev Mode, you can install additional extensions—including many popular Chromium extensions—for testing, development, and customization.

🎯

Session Management

By default, your session will automatically lock after 30 minutes of inactivity to guarantee safe browsing. This timer can be customized in settings for your preferred inactivity period.

Quick Start

  1. Download SVART for your platform
  2. Extract the archive
  3. Run: npm install
  4. Start: npm start
  5. Create your master password
  6. Set up your DNS provider (NextDNS recommended, affiliate link) and privacy options on the splash screen
  7. Browse securely using your alias or forwarding email for all accounts!
