ImprovMX Email Forwarding Setup

All email addresses above are forwarded via ImprovMX (free plan — 25 aliases, 1 destination).

Step 1 — Create an ImprovMX Account

  1. Go to improvmx.com and sign up (free).
  2. Click Add Domain and enter svartsecurity.org.
  3. Set your forwarding destination (personal inbox that will receive all mail).

Step 2 — Add DNS Records in Cloudflare

Go to Cloudflare Dashboard → svartsecurity.org → DNS → Records and add:

Type Name Value Priority
MX @ mx1.improvmx.com 10
MX @ mx2.improvmx.com 20
TXT @ v=spf1 include:spf.improvmx.com ~all —

Make sure Proxy status is set to DNS only (grey cloud) for MX records.

Step 3 — Create Aliases

In the ImprovMX dashboard, add each alias:

  • support → your inbox
  • contact → your inbox
  • security → your inbox
  • admin → your inbox
  • noreply → your inbox

Step 4 — Send As (Optional)

To reply from your @svartsecurity.org addresses via Gmail:

  1. Open Gmail → Settings → Accounts → Send mail as → Add another email.
  2. Enter the alias (e.g. support@svartsecurity.org).
  3. SMTP Server: smtp.improvmx.com, Port: 587, TLS.
  4. Username: your ImprovMX alias, Password: your ImprovMX API key (from Account Settings).
  5. Confirm the verification email sent to your inbox.

Note: SMTP sending via ImprovMX requires the Premium plan ($9.99/mo). On the free plan, you can receive and forward email but replies must come from your personal address.
