Email Management

Manage your custom email addresses and routing with Cloudflare.

Cloudflare Email Routing

Set up custom email addresses for your domain (e.g., you@svartsecurity.org) and forward them to your preferred inbox.

  1. Go to Cloudflare Dashboard.
  2. Select your domain and open Email > Email Routing.
  3. Add desired email addresses and set forwarding rules.

For advanced email management, connect your domain to a service like Mailgun or SendGrid for sending/receiving emails.
