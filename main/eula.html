4. Protection of Journalists & Press Freedom

Svart Security explicitly recognizes and supports the critical role of journalism in a free and democratic society. The following commitments form a core part of our product philosophy and this Agreement:

  • We will notify affected Users of data requests whenever legally permissible.

4.3 Commitment to International Press Freedom Standards

Svart Security's policies are guided by the principles set forth in:

  • Article 19 of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights (freedom of expression).
  • Article 10 of the European Convention on Human Rights (freedom of expression).
  • The First Amendment to the United States Constitution.
  • The UNESCO Recommendation on the Protection of Journalists.
  • International standards recognizing the confidentiality of journalistic sources.

4.4 Encryption by Default — Until Criminal Activity Is Detected

Svart Security's encryption protects every User equally, at all times, by default. Your data remains fully encrypted and inaccessible — including to us — for as long as you use the Software lawfully.

Our encryption and privacy protections are unconditional for lawful Users. Specifically:

  • Journalists, whistleblowers, and activists receive full, uncompromised encryption protection at all times.   • All other lawful Users likewise receive full encryption protection. Personal browsing, private communications, encrypted notes, document storage, and password management remain fully private and encrypted.
  • Criminal activity is the only exception. If Svart Security detects or becomes aware of illegal activity being conducted through or in connection with the Software — including but not limited to fraud, exploitation, trafficking, cyberattacks, distribution of CSAM, terrorism, or any other criminal conduct — a formal report will be generated and submitted to the appropriate law enforcement authorities.

To be absolutely clear:

If you are a journalist: You are protected. Full stop. Your encryption stays intact. No reports. No flags. No exceptions.

If you are engaged in criminal activity: A report will be made. Your use of the Software does not shield you from legal accountability. Privacy is a right — impunity is not.

This approach ensures that Svart Security remains a trusted tool for legitimate privacy while making it clear that criminals cannot hide behind our encryption. We protect people — we do not protect crimes.
