How It Works

Every account starts with Free Premium — 750 AI requests per month at £0. No credit card, no trial, no expiry. Requests reset on the 1st of each month.

Need more? Buy a top-up from as little as £0.50 for 500 requests — one-time payment, no subscription, no expiry. Requests stack and stay in your account until you use them.

When you launch Svart AI, it automatically detects your hardware (CPU cores, GPU type, available RAM) and selects the optimal LLM model for maximum speed. No configuration needed — it just works.

All plans include the same local LLM engine, AES-256-GCM encryption, internet scraping, custom training, and auto-detect optimisation. No feature gates, no ads on any plan. The only difference is request volume and support priority.

⚡

Auto-Detect Engine

Svart AI scans your CPU, GPU, and RAM on launch and automatically selects the best-performing LLM model for your exact hardware configuration. No manual setup.

Runs Locally

Every response is generated on your device. No network round-trip, no server queue, no “high demand” throttling. Your hardware speed IS the AI speed.

What Counts as a Request?

💬

AI Chat Messages

Each message you send to Svart AI counts as one request. The AI response is included — one request per prompt, not per token.

🌐

Web Scraping Queries

Asking Svart AI to search the internet for information counts as one request per query.

🧠

AI Training Sessions

Custom training prompts to teach your local LLM new information. Each training prompt is one request.

📄

Document Analysis

Asking Svart AI to analyse, summarise, or extract data from documents. Each analysis is one request.
