How It Works

Every account starts with Free Premium — 750 AI requests per month at £0. No credit card, no trial, no expiry. These reset on the 1st of each month. If you need more, upgrade to Svart AI at £4.99/mo for 2,000 requests (overflow £5 per 1,000), or Svart AI Unlimited at £9.99/mo for unlimited requests with priority processing.

All three plans include the full local LLM engine, AES-256-GCM encryption, internet scraping, and custom training. There are no feature gates between plans — no ads on any plan, ever. The only differences are request volume and support priority.

How your activation key works:
Each activation key generates its own unique API key for your Local LLM. This API key is random and only becomes active after you log in to your app with your local password. Your activation key is your ID for the server only — the server cannot see or access your API key or your data. This is why your encrypted AI is truly private and only you control it.

What Counts as an AI Request?

🤖

AI Processing Calls

Each call to the Svart AI engine — encryption, decryption, key derivation, or secure data processing — counts as one request.

🔒

Encrypt / Decrypt

AES-256-GCM encryption and decryption operations. Each operation is one request regardless of data size.

🔑

Key Derivation

PBKDF2 key derivation with 250,000 iterations. Generating or rotating keys each count as one request.

📈

Analytics Queries

Querying your AI usage dashboard or pulling analytics data. Viewing your dashboard is always free.
