How It Works

Every account starts with Free Premium — 750 AI chat requests per month at £0. No credit card, no trial, no expiry. Requests reset on the 1st of each month. If you need more, upgrade to Svart AI (£4.99/mo) for 2,000 requests, or Svart AI Unlimited (£9.99/mo) for unlimited.

All plans include the same local LLM engine, AES-256-GCM encryption, internet scraping, and custom training. No feature gates, no ads on any plan. The only difference is request volume and support priority.

What Counts as a Request?

💬

AI Chat Messages

Each message you send to Svart AI counts as one request. The AI response is included — it's one request per prompt, not per token.

🌐

Web Scraping Queries

Asking Svart AI to search the internet for information counts as one request per query.

🧠

AI Training Sessions

Custom training prompts to teach your local LLM new information. Each training prompt is one request.

📄

Document Analysis

Asking Svart AI to analyse, summarise, or extract data from documents. Each analysis is one request.

What Does NOT Count

🛠

All Other Svart Apps

🔒

Browsing & Ad Blocking

📈

Viewing Your Dashboard

Why We’re Cheaper

Svart AI runs on your device. We don’t pay per-query cloud compute costs — your machine does the work. Our servers only handle authentication and updates, and they can only see your activation key and email — never your data.

That’s why we can offer 750 free requests with the full engine and keep paid plans at half the price of every competitor.
