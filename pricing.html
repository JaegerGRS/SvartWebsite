Why We’re Cheaper AND Faster

Svart AI runs on your device. We don’t pay per-query cloud compute costs — your machine does the work. Our servers only handle authentication and updates, and they can only see your activation key and email — never your data.

Every cloud AI charges you for server time. OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Microsoft, and Perplexity all run your prompts on massive data centres and charge you for the privilege. You wait in their queue, you use their hardware, and they read every word you type.

Svart AI’s auto-detect engine scans your hardware on launch and selects the optimal model variant — right size for your GPU VRAM, right quantisation for your CPU, right context window for your available RAM. You get the fastest possible response from YOUR machine, not the slowest available slot on someone else’s.

That’s why we can offer 750 free requests with the full engine and keep paid plans at half the price of every competitor — while being faster.
