Top-Up FAQ

Do top-up requests expire?

No. Top-up requests never expire. They stay in your account until you use them, whether that’s tomorrow or in two years.

Can I buy multiple packs?

Yes. Buy as many packs as you want — they all stack together. Buy three £2.50 packs and you’ll have 7,500 requests added to your account.

Do top-ups work on the Free Premium plan?

Yes. Top-ups work on every plan including Free Premium. You get your 750 free monthly requests plus any top-ups you’ve purchased. Monthly requests are used first.

Is it a subscription?

No. Top-ups are a one-time payment. No auto-renewal, no recurring charges. You buy a pack, you get the requests. That’s it.

How quickly are requests added to my account?

Instantly. As soon as payment completes, the requests appear in your account and you can start using them immediately.

Are my monthly plan requests used first or top-up requests?

Monthly plan requests are used first. Your top-up balance is only consumed after your monthly allowance runs out. This means your top-ups last as long as possible.

What happens to unused monthly requests?

Monthly plan requests reset on the 1st of each month and do not roll over. Top-up requests are separate and never reset or expire.

Can I get a refund on unused top-up requests?

Since requests never expire, there’s no urgency to use them. Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis — contact support if you need help.

How do I check my top-up balance?

Your usage dashboard shows your current top-up balance, monthly allowance, and usage history. Go to Account → Dashboard to view it.

Why are Svart AI top-ups so much cheaper than competitors?

Svart AI runs on your device. We don’t pay per-query cloud compute costs — your machine does the work. That’s why we can offer requests at £0.001 each while cloud providers charge 10–40× more.
